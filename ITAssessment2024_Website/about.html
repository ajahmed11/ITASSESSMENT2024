About Our University

Since our founding in 1910, we’ve been expanding Kathmandu Professional University’s reach beyond the traditional student and conventional classroom. What began as a series of courses for the local community has evolved into an accredited Harvard school serving adult learners around the globe, from over 120 countries.

As one of 12 degree-granting schools at Kathmandu Professional University, we teach the largest and most eclectic student body at the University. Our students come to us from various time zones and cultural and professional backgrounds.
