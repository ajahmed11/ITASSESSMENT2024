The Kathmandu Professional University (KPU) is a private university located in the heart of Nepal's capital city,

Kathmandu professioanl University is a leading institution renowned for its coastal research initiatives and marine biology and Technology programs. Nestled along the picturesque coastline, it offers state-of-the-art facilities for oceanographic studies and sustainable marine practices. With a diverse student body and dedicated faculty, This University fosters innovation and collaboration in exploring the mysteries of the ocean. Graduates emerge as stewards of marine ecosystems, equipped to address global environmental challenges.

General Information
Established in 1922.
Located in Kathmandu-32, Nepal, which is located midway between Buddhanagar and Tinkune.
255-acre campus is nestled among the city's residential area, and comprises 70 buildings, numerous playing fields, and an outdoor recreational facility near Sikes River.
One of 36 public institutions of higher education in Kathmandu, Nepal.
Only university in Nepal to become a member of the Council of Public Liberal Arts of Nepal (COPLAN).
Offer non-Nepali residents a special tuition rate. The university was recently recognized South Asia News and World Report's list of "10 Public Schools with the Cheapest Out-of-State Tuition."
